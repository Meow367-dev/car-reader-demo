CheckLight — Support

Which adapters work?

Any ELM327-compatible OBD-II adapter. On iPhone, use a Bluetooth LE or WiFi adapter (the box should say "works with iOS"). On Android, Bluetooth, WiFi, and USB-C adapters all work. Recommended: Vgate iCar Pro BLE, OBDLink MX+.

Where is the OBD-II port?

Under the dashboard on the driver's side, usually within a foot of the steering column. Every gas car sold in the US since 1996 has one.

The app won't connect

  • Make sure the ignition is on (engine running or accessory mode).
  • Check the adapter is seated firmly in the port.
  • For Bluetooth adapters: pair inside the CheckLight app, not in your phone's Bluetooth settings.
