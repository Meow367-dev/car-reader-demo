Car Reader — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

Car Reader reads diagnostic information from your vehicle and explains it in plain English. We built this policy to be readable the same way.

What we collect

  • Vehicle diagnostic data — trouble codes, sensor readings, freeze-frame data, and your vehicle identification number (VIN), read from your car when you connect an OBD-II adapter.
  • Account information — your email address and name if you create an account.
  • Repair contributions — if you choose to share what fixed a problem and what it cost, that report is stored with your vehicle's make, model, and year.

How we use it

  • To show you your car's diagnostics and history across your devices.
  • To build anonymous, aggregate repair statistics (for example, "most drivers fixed this code with new spark plugs"). Aggregate statistics never include your name, email, VIN, or location.
  • To improve vehicle coverage and diagnostic accuracy.

What we don't do

  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • We do not share your identity with other users. Community contributions appear under a username you choose.
  • We do not track your location.

Subscriptions

Payments are processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We never see or store your payment card details.

Data deletion

You can delete your account and associated personal data at any time from Settings inside the app, or by emailing us.
